The Why

Cyclum Renewables microgrids will improve Carbon Intensity Score of Renewable Natural Gas (RNG) by powering GESS Biogas and AL’s biogas facilities. Electricity consumption for the digester and gas upgrading process is a component of the carbon intensity rating attached to their RNG production

The government incentivizes installing solar using the Solar Investment Tax Credit (ITC). The ITC is currently 26%, and will decrease in proceeding years. The IRS offers a “Safe Harbor” option which extends the window of receiving an ITC rate from 12 months to 48 months. Projects are eligible of using the ITC rate of the year preceding and proceeding the current year

Main idea

Carbon Intensity is the measure of CO2 emitted from consuming energy.

Lowering Carbon Intensity is incentivized through Low Carbon Fuel Standard Credits (LCFS)

Utilizing Cyclum Renewables’ microgrids is a definite method of obtaining LCFS Credits for the biogas plants, leading to increased revenue.
